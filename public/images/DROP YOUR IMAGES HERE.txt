  DROP YOUR 6 IMAGES INTO THIS FOLDER
======================================

You can use ANY filename — no renaming needed!

Your images will be assigned to the 6 scenes
in alphabetical order:

  1st image (alphabetically) → Scene 1: Prophecy / Scroll
  2nd image                  → Scene 2: Jerusalem 70 AD
  3rd image                  → Scene 3: Spain / Portugal
  4th image                  → Scene 4: West Africa
  5th image                  → Scene 5: Ancient Maps
  6th image                  → Scene 6: Slavery / Ships

TIP: To control which image goes to which scene,
     rename them so they sort in the right order.
     Example:  1-scroll.png, 2-jerusalem.png, etc.
     (Windows: right-click → Rename)

OR just use the exact scene names if you prefer:
  scene-01-scroll.png
  scene-02-jerusalem.png
  scene-03-spain.png
  scene-04-westafrica.png
  scene-05-maps.png
  scene-06-slavery.png
